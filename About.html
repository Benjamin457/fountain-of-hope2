Vision

  • To raise leaders who will show God's love by using their skills and expertise to equip the less previlaged in Africa with skills that are economically as way of helping to eradicate poverty and illeteracy

    Minister to the spiritual needs through preaching God's word by evangelism, descipleship as well releasing people into church planting missions

    Care for the mind and soul of people by running skills development programmes for children and adults in a form of after school programmes, pre schools and couseling sessions.

    Care for the body by running feeding programmes, home based care programmes, orphans and vulnerable children programmes as well as social working at large


    Mission

  • Fountain of Hope Church exists to demonstrate Jesus love by caring for the vulnerable in the community where it is established.
